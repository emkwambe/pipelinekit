Pricing Principles



Every pricing experiment must align with:



Predictability

Customer trust

Simplicity

Transparency

Sustainable economics



Experiments that violate these principles should be rejected regardless of revenue impact.

Experiments We Will Not Run



PipelineKit explicitly rejects the following pricing models.



Usage Pricing



Examples:



Rows processed

Data volume

Pipeline executions



Reason:



Violates pricing philosophy.



Compute Pricing



Reason:



Customer already pays cloud provider.



Connector Pricing



Reason:



Punishes integration adoption.



Opaque Enterprise Pricing



Reason:



Reduces trust.



Decision Rules



Pricing experiments should only be adopted when they improve at least one of:



Revenue

Retention

Expansion

Customer satisfaction



without materially harming:



Trust

Simplicity

Predictability

Success Metrics



PipelineKit pricing should optimize for:



Retention

>

Expansion

>

Acquisition

>

Revenue Maximization



Long-term trust is more valuable than short-term revenue.
