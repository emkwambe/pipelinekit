Status: Foundational Business Document

Date: June 24, 2026

Purpose



This document defines how PipelineKit generates revenue.



The objective is not merely to maximize revenue.



The objective is to maximize:



Customer trust

Customer retention

Product adoption

Long-term company value



Revenue should be the result of customer success.



Not the result of customer dependency.



Revenue Philosophy



PipelineKit is fundamentally an infrastructure company.



Infrastructure companies fail when they monetize friction.



Infrastructure companies succeed when they monetize value.



PipelineKit will monetize:



Expertise

Trust

Productivity

Governance

Diagnostics



PipelineKit will not monetize:



Data volume

Pipeline executions

Rows processed

Compute consumption



These belong to customer infrastructure providers.
