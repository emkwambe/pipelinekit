Purpose



This document defines the economic model required for PipelineKit to become a sustainable software company.



The goal is not merely revenue growth.



The goal is profitable, repeatable, scalable growth.



Every future pricing, product, and customer acquisition decision should be evaluated against these economics.



Economic Philosophy



PipelineKit is fundamentally a software business.



Software scales.



Services do not.



Services may accelerate learning during the early stages of the company, but the long-term objective is recurring software revenue.



Success requires:



High Retention

\+

Low Support Cost

\+

Efficient Customer Acquisition

\+

Predictable Revenue

Primary Economic Unit



The primary unit is:



Annual Recurring Revenue Per Customer



Not:



Pipelines

Users

Connectors

API Calls



Customers purchase trusted analytics outcomes.



Revenue should be measured at the customer level.
