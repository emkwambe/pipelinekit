# **SECTION 1**

# **Business Model & Revenue Strategy**

## **Core Principle**

PipelineKit is not a software company first.

PipelineKit is a blueprint company.

Software enables blueprint delivery.

Blueprints create value.

Support creates revenue.

---

### **Revenue Stream 1**

Subscription

Products:

* Team  
* Business  
* Enterprise

70% of long-term revenue
